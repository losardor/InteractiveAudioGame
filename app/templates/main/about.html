About Soundmaze

We believe stories are best told out loud.

What is Soundmaze

Soundmaze is an interactive audiobook platform where every listener is also a protagonist. Stories branch at key moments — and the path you choose is yours alone.

Our Mission

We're building the missing middle ground between passive audiobooks and screen-heavy games. An experience that respects your attention, works without looking at your phone, and puts the story first.

Where We Are

Soundmaze is currently in early development. The platform is live and accepting its first readers. If you'd like to get in touch, write to us at hello@soundmaze.it.
